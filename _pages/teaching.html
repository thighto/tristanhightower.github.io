I have worked as a teaching assistant for two courses:
  • GVPT 202: Politics, Constitutional Policy, and the Institution of the U.S. Supreme Court (Patrick Wohlfarth, Primary Instructor), Spring 2019.
  • GVPT 473: The U.S. Congress (Ric Uslaner, Primary Instructor), Fall 2018.
For both courses, I was the highest ranked teaching assistant in the department based on aggregate course evaluation scores. I also ranked in the top percentiles in the college and university. If you would like a copy of my course evaluations, please contact me.
